Our Himalayan Heritage

Founded by a team of passionate agriculture researcher, botanist and conservationists, The Plant Hub of Himalayan Nepal is dedicated to preserving and sharing the diversity of the majestic Himalayan region.

We work directly with local communities to sustainably harvest and propagate rare plant species, ensuring their survival for generations to come while supporting local livelihoods.

Our commitment to sustainability

Every plant in our collection is ethically sourced and cultivated with respect for nature and local communities.
